Smart Parking

Smart Parking is a system that uses digital technology and sensors to monitor and manage vehicle parking more efficiently. The system provides real-time information about parking space availability to drivers via mobile phone apps or electronic signs, helping to save time and fuel that would normally be wasted in searching for empty parking spaces. The main goal is to improve the driver experience and reduce traffic congestion in urban areas.

How It Works

Smart Parking works by using sensors placed in each parking space to continuously monitor its status. These sensors send data about parking space availability to a central system via wireless communication. This information is then conveyed to motorists via smartphone applications or electronic boards that show empty parking spaces by color code. With this guide, motorists can easily find available parking spaces, saving time and reducing frustration in searching for parking. The Smart Parking system also helps parking managers to optimize the use of parking areas and increase operational efficiency, making it one of the solutions to overcome the increasing urban parking problem.
