Connected Car

Connected Car, or connected car, refers to a car or motorized vehicle that is equipped with communication technology and an internet connection. This technology allows vehicles to connect to cellular networks or the internet through various devices such as cellular modems, GPS, sensors and software interfaces. Connected cars enable data exchange between vehicles, drivers and traffic infrastructure, which enables a variety of additional functions and services.

How It Works

Connected Car works by utilizing communication technology and internet connectivity that is integrated in the vehicle. The vehicle is equipped with hardware such as a cellular modem, GPS, sensors and software that enables it to connect to a cellular network or the internet. Through this connection, vehicles can send and receive data in real-time. This enables various functions, such as navigation with traffic updates, monitoring of vehicle conditions, and entertainment services connected to online content. In addition, vehicles can also communicate with traffic infrastructure and other vehicles, assisting in smarter navigation and contributing to the development of autonomous vehicles. Connected Car allows drivers to have a safer, more efficient and connected driving experience with the outside world.
